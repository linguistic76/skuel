
  The three-tier pattern solves real problems for ~30% of your models. Don't force it on the other 70% where it adds no value.


  The Reality 🎯

  The three-tier pattern is valuable for complex domains but overkill for simple ones:

  Worth It For:

  - ✅ Task, Event, Knowledge, Progress - Complex business logic, state management
  - ✅ Route handlers - Pydantic validation is genuinely useful

  NOT Worth It For:

  - ❌ Journal, Finance, Goal, Principle - Simple CRUD, no complex logic
  - ❌ Utility services - Internal tools don't need this complexity
