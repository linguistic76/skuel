# Reflective Essay — How Breath Shapes Attention

## Overview

You have been practicing breath meditation for two weeks. This assignment asks you
to look inward and describe what you've actually experienced — not what you think
you *should* have experienced.

## What to Write

Write 300-500 words reflecting on these questions:

1. **The moment of noticing** — Describe a specific time during practice when you
   realized your mind had wandered. What brought you back? How did it feel?

2. **Breath as anchor** — In your own words, explain what it means to use breath
   as an anchor for attention. Use your actual experience, not definitions.

3. **Beyond the cushion** — Has your meditation practice affected your attention
   in daily life? Give one concrete example (studying, conversations, sports, etc.).

## What Makes a Strong Response

- Honesty over eloquence — say what actually happened, not what sounds impressive
- Specificity — "Tuesday morning I noticed..." beats "Sometimes I notice..."
- Self-awareness — noticing patterns in your own mind IS the practice

## Submission

Submit as a journal entry. You may submit up to 2 times before the due date.
Your teacher will review your submission and may request a revision with feedback.
