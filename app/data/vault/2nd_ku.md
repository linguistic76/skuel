## Why Breath?

Breath is the anchor of attention. When the mind wanders, return to breath.

## Core Practice

1. Sit comfortably with spine upright
2. Close eyes or soften gaze
3. Notice the breath at the nostrils or belly
4. When attention drifts, gently return — no judgment

## Key Insight

The practice is not "staying focused." The practice is *noticing you drifted and returning*.
That moment of noticing IS the meditation.
